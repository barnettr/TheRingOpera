National Patron Program

Chairs The Honorable and Mrs. George P. Shultz

For our friends traveling from outside Northern California—receive special attention and VIP service while in town for your Ring experience.

$5,000 National Patron Silver Circle

 
  • Complimentary parking for all performances
  • Recognition in performance programs
  • Priority seating for up to two Orchestra level Ring tickets
  • Valhalla Lounge access
  • Complimentary VIP pass to lectures during your stay
  • Invitation to participate in a Behind the Scenes event
  • Invitation to a Das Rheingold champagne reception in Valhalla Lounge
  • Commemorative San Francisco Opera Ring lapel pin

$10,000 National Patron Gold Circle All above benefits plus:

 
  • Priority seating for up to two Ring tickets in Box seats
  • Intimate reception in a private home with General Director David Gockley and Maestro Donald Runnicles exclusively for National Patrons
  • Invitation to iconic San Francisco cultural events

In addition, San Francisco Opera will work with partner hotels and a partner airline to accommodate out-of-town guests.

For further information or questions, please contact Marissa Axell at (415) 551-6239 or maxell@sfopera.com

In accordance with contribution guidelines and San Francisco Opera policy, please note the following:

The contribution portion of Ring cycle ticket prices will be recognized in program listings and is eligible for benefits during the season the Ring is performed: August 2010–July 2011.

Ring Circle gifts and Ring production sponsors will be recognized in program listings and are eligible for benefits during the season the Ring is performed: August 2010–July 2011. Additionally, these gifts will be recognized on a special Ring page of the program book during both the 2009–10 and the 2010–11 seasons.
