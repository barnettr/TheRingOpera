Francesca Zambello, Director

  • ”The themes in the Ring—greed, corruption, love, destruction of the environment—are integral to the work and to human instinct. Our challenge was to reflect Wagner’s themes into a visual world that is parallel with our own. The production has both a realistic edge and a fantastical dimension. Our hope is that our audience will intuit the connections to the world of today while fully entering into the realm of myth.”
